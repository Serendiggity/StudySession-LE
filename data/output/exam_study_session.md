## Topic: Discharge Process

### Q: What type of discharge is available where no facts proven under BIA s. 173?
**Answer:** A. An absolute discharge
**Section:** 172(1)(a)
**Quote:** "On the hearing of an application of a bankrupt for a discharge, other than a bankrupt referred to in section 172.1, the court may (a) grant or refuse an absolute order of discharge"
**Cross-refs:** Section 173 (facts requiring alternative discharge), Section 178 (debts surviving discharge)
**Rationale:** Section 172(2) mandates refusal/suspension/conditional discharge only when s.173 facts are proven. Where NO s.173 facts exist, section 172(1)(a) applies, giving court discretion to grant absolute discharge.
